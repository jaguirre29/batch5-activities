Paul Walker

A Fast and the Furious franchise Actor

Here is a timeline of Paul Walker life.

  • 1973 - Born September 12, 1973 in Glendale, California, U.S.
  • 1970 - Walker began his career as a child actor during the 1970s and 1980s
  • 1990 - Gained recognition after appearing in the television soap opera The Young and the Restless.
  • 2001 - Gained international fame after starring in The Fast and the Furious
  • 2005 - Performances in box-office disappointments Into the Blue and
  • 2006 - Running Scared
  • 2010 - Appeared in low budget action films, but starred in the commercially successful heist film takers
  • 2013 - Walker died on November 30, 2013 at the age of 40 as a passenger in a single-car crash alongside friend and driver Roger Rodas thought to be caused by driving into a gas pipe.
